## Escalation Procedures

### Level 1: Stakeholder Disagreement
**Trigger:** Consensus not reached at validation checkpoint  
**Resolution:** Project Owner mediation session within 24 hours  
**Timeline:** 1 additional business day  

### Level 2: Technical Validation Failure  
**Trigger:** Pilot testing reveals significant issues  
**Resolution:** Technical review board assessment  
**Timeline:** 2-3 additional business days  
**Fallback:** Rollback to previous stable state

### Level 3: Executive Escalation
**Trigger:** Project Owner cannot resolve stakeholder conflicts  
**Resolution:** Executive Sponsor final decision  
**Timeline:** 1-2 additional business days  
**Authority:** Final decision on integration proceed/cancel

## Communication Plan

#### Daily Status Updates  
**Recipients:** All stakeholders

#### Milestone Completions
**Recipients:** Project Owner, All stakeholders

**Recipients:** Escalation path participants + Project Owner
